Brand Name:

Synthroid

Generic Name:

Levothyroxine

Uses:

Synthetic thyroid hormone that is used to treat a condition called hypothyroidism. It is intended to replace a hormone that is normally produced by your thyroid gland. Generally, thyroid replacement therapy is to be taken for life.

Possible Side Effects

Continued hypothyroidism, Rapid or irregular heartbeat, Chest pain or shortness of breath, Muscle weakness, Nervousness, Irritability, Sleeplesness, Tremors, Change in appetite, Weight Loss, Vommiting, Fredquent bowl movements, Excessive sweating, Heat intolerance, Fever, Impaired fertility, Decreased bone density, Changes in menstrual periods, Other unusual medical events
