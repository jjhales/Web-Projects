Welcome to RX Lookup!

RX Lookup is a simple medication lookup service. You enter a medication name in the search box, brand name or generic is accepted, and the results will show brand name, generic name, uses, and possible side effects. It can also produce results with other medications that can be used. You can either end your experience right there or become a member of our site. Sign up is simple and will allow you to save and catergorize medications for later. Becoming a member also allows you to browse our medication database by category.

Save medications

As a member you will be able to save medications that you search and catergorize them. You can also add your own notes for your doctor later.

Persons

Add people such as children, spouses, patients and doctors to your profile and assign medications to them.

Scheduling

Schedule dosages, appointments then set email or text alerts to remind you when to take your medication or another person.
